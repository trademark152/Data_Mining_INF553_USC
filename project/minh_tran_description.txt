Method Description:
The method utilizes the hybrid approach from the open-sourced Surprise package. The link to the package is here: https://surprise.readthedocs.io/en/stable/index.html. Two approaches are combined with equal weights. They are baseline method and SVD (Singular value decomposition) (https://surprise.readthedocs.io/en/stable/matrix_factorization.html)
